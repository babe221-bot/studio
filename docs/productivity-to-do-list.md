## ðŸ’¡ Productivity Tips

1. **Start with the hardest task** - Tackle your most challenging task when energy is highest
2. **Use time blocking** - Dedicate specific time blocks for focused work
3. **Batch similar tasks** - Group related tasks to maintain flow
4. **Take breaks** - Use the Pomodoro Technique (25 min work + 5 min break)
5. **Review weekly** - Assess progress and adjust priorities every week
6. **Limit daily goals** - Focus on 3-5 major accomplishments per day
7. **Eliminate before adding** - Remove tasks before adding new ones
