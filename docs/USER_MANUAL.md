# User Manual: Intelligent Designer

Welcome to the next generation of stone fabrication design. This manual will help you master the "Lab" experience.

## 1. Starting a Design
Select a "Tip elementa" (Element type) to load standard dimensions.
*   **Piece:** Individual custom slabs.
*   **Sqm:** Area-based pricing (facades, flooring).
*   **Lm:** Linear-based pricing (window sills, plinths).

## 2. Using the Intelligent Assistant
Click the chat icon to talk to the AI.
*   **Ask:** "Is this slab too heavy for a regular cabinet?"
*   **Command:** "Change the material to black granite and make it 2 meters long."

## 3. Visual Configuration
*   **3D Visualization:** Rotate and zoom to inspect your design.
*   **Grain Alignment:** (Experimental) Use the sliders to move the texture around until the veins look perfect.
*   **AR View:** Click "Pripremi 3D model" then scan the QR code (on desktop) or click "View in Space" (on mobile) to see the slab in your actual room.

## 4. Finalizing the Order
1.  Click **"Dodaj stavku u nalog"** (Add to order).
2.  Review your list in section 5.
3.  Click **"Preuzmi Nalog (PDF)"** to get a branded quotation with manufacturing drawings.
